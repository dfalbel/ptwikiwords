Survivor Series (1997) foi o décimo-primeiro evento anual do Survivor Series, promovido pela World Wrestling Federation e transmitido por pay-per-view. Aconteceu dia 9 de Novembro de 1997 no Molson Centre em Montreal, Quebec.Foi onde aconteceu o Montreal Screwjob.

# Resultados Estipulações Tempos
1 The New Age Outlaws (Billy Gunn e Road Dogg) e The Godwinns (Henry e Phineas) derrotaram The Headbangers (Mosh e Thrasher) e The New Blackjacks (Blackjack Windham e Blackjack Bradshaw). Four-on-four Survivor Series elimination match1 15:27
2 The Truth Commission (The Jackal, The Interrogator, Sniper, e Recon) venceram The Disciples of Apocalypse (Crush, Chainz, 8-Ball, e Skull). Four-on-four Survivor Series elimination match2 09:59
3 Team Canada (The British Bulldog, Jim Neidhart, Doug Furnas e Phil Lafon) derrotaram Team USA (Vader, Goldust, Marc Mero e Steve Blackman) (c/Sable). Four-on-four Survivor Series elimination match3 17:47
4 Kane (c/ Paul Bearer) venceu Mankind. Luta individual 09:29
5 The Road Warriors (Hawk e Animal), Ahmed Johnson e Ken Shamrock) derrotaram The Nation of Domination (The Rock, Faarooq, Kama Mustafa e D'Lo Brown). Four-on-four Survivor Series elimination match4 20:34
6 Steve Austin venceu Owen Hart (c) (c/ The British Bulldog, Jim Neidhart, Doug Furnas e Phil Lafon). Luta individual pelo WWF Intercontinental Championship 04:04
7 Shawn Michaels derrotou Bret Hart (c)., graças ao famoso Montreal Screwjob Luta individual pelo WWF Championship 12:11

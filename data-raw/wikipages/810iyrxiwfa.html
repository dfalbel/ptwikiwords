Survivor Series (1994) foi o 8º evento anual do Survivor Series, promovido pela World Wrestling Federation e transmitido por pay-per-view. Aconteceu dia 23 de Novembro de 1994 no Freeman Coliseum em San Antonio, Texas.

Resultados[editar | editar código-fonte]

# Resultados Estipulações Tempos
1 The Bad Guys (Razor Ramon, The 1-2-3 Kid, The British Bulldog, Fatu e Sionne) (c/ Afa e Captain Lou Albano) derrotaram The Teamsters (Shawn Michaels, Diesel, Owen Hart, Jim Neidhart e Jeff Jarrett).[1][2] Five-on-five Survivor Series elimination match1 21:45
2 The Royal Family (Jerry Lawler, Sleazy, Queasy and Cheesy) venceram Clowns R' Us (Doink the Clown, Dink, Pink e Wink).[1][2][3] Four-on-four Survivor Series elimination match2 16:05
3 Bob Backlund (c/ Owen Hart) derrotou Bret Hart (c/ The British Bulldog).[1][2][4] Submission match pelo WWF Championship 35:11
4 The Million Dollar Team (King Kong Bundy, Tatanka e Bam Bam Bigelow, Jimmy Del Ray e Tom Prichard) (c/ Ted DiBiase) venceram Guts and Glory (Lex Luger, Mabel, Adam Bomb, Billy Gunn e Bart Gunn) (c/ Oscar).[1] Five-on-five Survivor Series elimination match3 23:21
5 The Undertaker (c/ Paul Bearer e Chuck Norris) derrotou Yokozuna (c/ Mr. Fuji e Jim Cornette).[1][2][5][6]

Casket match com Chuck Norris como juiz especial

 15:24
